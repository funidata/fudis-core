Fudis is Funidata's evolving Design System

Fudis is Funidata's evolving Design System, an open and accessible foundation for building accessible and sustainable learning services. Fudis enables responsible and efficient service development through styles and components, promoting equity in learning services. Currently, more detailed design guidelines are maintained on Funidata's private Figma account.
